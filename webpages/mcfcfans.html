About the Club

MUMBAI CITY FC is one the teams playing in the Indian Super League. In terms of profile this team had many well known and best overseases player in their team wearing the blue and golden jersey. Recently one of the great things happened this club is that the City Football Group has now bought 80% of the club's stake which make them the owner of this club. City Football Group also own the Manchester City, Girona, Melbourne fc and few others.

About stadium

Mumbai City Fc's Home stadium is "Mumbai football arena".
This stadium is located in Andheri,Mumbai. Mumbai are playing here since the last three seasons. This stadium is one of the few stadiums which is a football dedicated stadium and not a multi-sport stadium. Although the stadium's capacity is just around 9k, This stadium offers one of the best views for the spactaters inside the stadium as the stands are much closer to the pitch.
